ABOUT US

Durga Textiles is a well-established and a leading Handkerchief manufacturing company, incorporated in the year 2010, under the headship of Mr Ashok Shah. The company is based in Mumbai (India) and it operates with a strong supply-chain network across India. We are in the business of manufacturing & supplying the premium quality handkerchiefs for Men as well as women across India. Bringing superiority is the forte of Durga Textiles, thus our products are made using 100% quality acceptable fabric to safeguard a high degree of comfortability and uniqueness. Our complete range of handkerchief is inspected on different quality precincts assuring no scope of flaws and complaints.
